Privacy Policy

Internal office Q&A Management App · Last updated: 2026-05-26

1. What this app stores

This internal app may store your name, work email, Google profile information, submitted questions, answers, comments, team chat messages, notifications, role assignments, and activity records needed to operate the service.

2. Why the data is used

The data is used to manage office questions, support reviewer workflows, run team chat, show activity history, and help admins maintain the app.

3. Who can access the data

Access is controlled by Supabase authentication and database Row Level Security policies. Staff, reviewers, admins, and primary admins have different permissions.

4. File attachments

The current app supports small inline attachments. For production, use approved company storage or Supabase Storage for larger files rather than storing large file data directly in question/chat text.

5. Contact

Contact the app administrator for access, correction, deletion, or security questions.
