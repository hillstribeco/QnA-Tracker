Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Company: HillsTribe Tech Solutions Country: Nepal App: Data Entry Q&A Tracker Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

HillsTribe Tech Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Data Entry Q&A Tracker application available at de-qna-tracker.netlify.app.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the App.

This is an internal tool used exclusively by HillsTribe Tech Solutions staff members.

2. Information We Collect

From Google Sign-In:

  • Your full name
  • Your Google email address
  • Your Google profile picture (display only)

When you submit a question:

  • Bill ID
  • Question text
  • Issue field category
  • Optional links or screenshots
  • Submission date and time

We do NOT collect:

  • Passwords
  • Payment information
  • Location data
  • Browsing history

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To identify you and display your name on submitted questions
  • To send email notifications when your question is received or answered
  • To allow reviewers to respond to your questions
  • To maintain a searchable knowledge base for the team

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely using Supabase on servers in Singapore. We use Row Level Security to protect your data.

5. Data Sharing

We only share data with these service providers:

  • Supabase — database hosting
  • EmailJS — previously used, now removed
  • Google — authentication
  • Netlify — app hosting

6. Your Rights

  • Access your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
